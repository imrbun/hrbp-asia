Frequently Asked Questions

How can I purchase tickets for your events?


Step One: To start the registration process, please complete the registration form available on each event page. If you prefer, you may also contact our team to request the form in Excel format.

Step Two: Once we receive your completed registration form, a member of our team will reach out to confirm your participation and provide any additional details you may need.

Step Three: Our Finance Department will then issue an invoice based on the information provided in your registration. If you have any specific invoicing requirements, just let us know—we’re happy to accommodate to ensure a smooth experience.

How to make payment?


Full payment must be received within seven (7) working days upon receipt of invoice or before the event date, whichever is earlier. A letter of undertaking (LOU) with the company stamp is requested for those organizations that cannot make payment on time.

(1) BANK TRANSFER
Please refer to the invoice for bank account details, different bank account will be provided based on your payment currencies.

(2) CREDIT CARD
Please take note we are using Stripe as the credit card payment gateway. There is an additional 3.9% + SGD0.5 credit card charges per transaction.

What is included in the event registration fee?


The registration fee includes:
• Access to the event sessions
• Printed training materials for in-person training courses
• Examination and certification (for applicable certification courses)
• Lunch and two refreshment breaks for all in-person classes (unless stated otherwise in the event brochure)

Please refer to the event brochure for any specific exceptions or details.
Note: Hotel accommodation and travel expenses are not included in the registration fee. However, we offer corporate rates with the event venue. For more information, please contact us at enquiry@hrbp-asia.com.

Is there any deadline for registration?


There is no deadline for registration. However, for some courses, sometimes we do limit the number of delegates to ensure optimum course interaction. We advise you to register as early as possible in order to avoid disappointment and to allow adequate time for our administration process.

Cancellation, Substitution & Refund Policy


• Substitution: If a registered delegate is unable to attend, a substitute participant is welcome at no additional charge. Please inform us in advance of any substitutions.
• Full Refund: A full refund will be issued for cancellations made at least 30 days prior to the event.
• 50% Refund: Cancellations received less than 30 days before the event will be eligible for a 50% refund.
• No Refund: Cancellations or requests for postponement made within 7 days of the event are not eligible for a refund or deferral.

All cancellation and refund requests must be made in writing and sent to the designated contact person or email address. Refunds will be processed within a specified timeframe upon receiving the request. Please note: This is a general policy. For specific terms and conditions, kindly refer to the event brochure or registration form. In the event of any discrepancies, the terms outlined in the brochure or registration form will apply.

Other Enquiries

Can I get a copy of the delegate list prior to the event?


We do not share delegate contact details. However, a list of attending companies can be provided upon request. We encourage you to bring your business cards for networking opportunities during the event.

Can I get a copy of the course materials prior to the event for self-study?


Yes, for some courses. Please note that we update course materials closer to the event to ensure all content is current. Materials will typically be shared about one week before the event, and full payment is required to receive them in advance.

What is the dress code for the event?


Most delegates opt for business or smart casual attire. Please avoid wearing shorts. You may also bring formal clothing if you plan to attend dinners or networking functions.

Certificate of Attendance


A Certificate of Attendance will be provided for all training courses. For conferences and forums, certificates are available upon request.

Who should I inform about my dietary requirements?


We will send an event information email approximately two weeks prior to the event. You can share any dietary restrictions by replying to that email.

Language & Interpretation


All public events are conducted in English, unless otherwise stated. English subtitles are available for select online courses.

Can I get the speakers’ presentation slides for conferences or forums?


Yes, presentation slides will be shared within one week after the event, but only for sessions where speakers have agreed to share their content. Sensitive or proprietary information may be removed.
